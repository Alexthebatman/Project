Artemis 1

Launch: Nov 16th, 2022

Mission Duration: 25 Earth days

Retired: N/A

Launched on November 16, 2022, Artemis 1 marked NASA's first mission in the Artemis program, using the powerful Space Launch System (SLS) rocket. Carrying the Orion spacecraft, it orbited the Moon, testing critical systems without crew onboard, paving the way for future human exploration, including missions aiming for lunar landings and beyond.
